My Work

RECIPE CREATION TOOL
Encouraging home cooks to share their recipes within the Kitchen Stories community

ROLE: Product Designer
TEAM: 1 Product Designer 6 Engineers
PROJECT TYPE: Mobile (Android & IOS)
YEAR: 2021

Kitchen Stories is a digital cooking platform with content being created by both our editors and users. The product an tech team developed a recipe creation tool that enabled users to share their recipes with 3 million fellow cooking enthusiasts on the Kitchen stories app.

FEATURE OVERVIEW

Creating a recipe
The main feature of this project was to allow the user to publish their recipe on the Kitchen Stories platform through a series of steps. The creation process was designed so that the output would be in the same format as existing Kitchen Stories recipes.

Author Attributes
Prior to launch, dish tiles had no assigned author because all recipes were simply developed by the in-house test kitchen. With user recipes now being added to the mix, there was a need to give credit to authors and differentiate recipes that are by Kitchen Stories, brand partners and the community of users.
